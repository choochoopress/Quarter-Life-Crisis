FEELINGS

Anxiety, in short, is the existential canary in our inner cage—the vital sign reminding us not just that we are alive, but that we are also alive to the fact that we can choose how to be and who we are. 1
May I, composed like them Of Eros and of dust, Beleaguered by the same Negation and despair, Show an affirming flame. 2
SOFTENED by Time’s consummate plush, How sleek the woe appears That threatened childhood’s citadel And undermined the years! Bisected now by bleaker griefs, We envy the despair That devastated childhood’s realm, So easy to repair. 3
